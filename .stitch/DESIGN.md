# Design System for FoundIt AI

**Vibe & Mood:**
Minimal, aesthetic, modern, clean, software-as-a-service feel. Friendly but secure. No clutter. Generous whitespace, card-based layouts.

**Platform:** Web (responsive desktop-first but mobile-friendly)

**Theme:** 
Light mode focused, with a soft cool-toned color palette.

**Colors:**
- Primary Action: Soft Indigo / Deep Blue (`#4F46E5`) for primary buttons and accents.
- Background: Very light pearl gray or off-white (`#F9FAFB`) to make cards pop.
- Surface / Cards: Pure white (`#FFFFFF`) with subtle soft shadows for elevation.
- Success Accent: Soft Emerald Green (`#10B981`) for match / success states.
- Error/Warning Accent: Rose Red (`#EF4444`) for lost item alerts.
- Text Primary: Dark Slate (`#1F2937`) for high legibility.
- Text Secondary: Medium Gray (`#6B7280`) for hints, timestamps, subtitles.

**Typography:**
- Inter or modern sans-serif. Thick and bold for headers, legible and crisp for body text.

**Components:**
- Cards: Gently rounded corners (16px / `1rem`), soft drop shadow, generous inner padding.
- Buttons: Pill shaped or fully rounded corners (9999px), smooth hover transitions.
- Inputs: Light gray background (`#F3F4F6`), subtle border, rounds corners (8px), expands or highlights border on focus.
- Icons: Minimal, line-based icons (Feather or Lucide style).

**Layout:**
- Centralized content column for forms.
- Sidebar or top navigation with clean distinct sections.
- Spacing: Systematic use of large margins to prevent crowded UIs.
